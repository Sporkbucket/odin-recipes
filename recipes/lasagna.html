Lasagna

Description

Do you hate Mondays? I sure don't! On the rare occasion that I do feel like a Grumpy Gertie, nothing cheers me up like a big dollop of lasagna. Geeze, I guess I could just make it a low-carb lasagne! | The Atkins Diet is an extreme low carbohydrate, low fat, and high-fat diet for weight loss and long-term health.

The Atkins diet works by dramatically reducing carbohydrates and increasing fats and protein. Once you've completed the diet, you're left with about 2 cups of liquid. No carb counting, no counting carbs, just plain good old fashioned carbs. As for your eating habits, the keto diet makes no sense to me. Ingredients

  • Tomato-based meat sauce
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Red wine
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Set your oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Boil the noodles if necessary.
  3. Build it in layers.
  4. Put it in the oven.
  5. Bake until done, or until Monday is almost over.
  6. Drink the wine.
