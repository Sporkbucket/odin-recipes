Spagett

Description

When I was a little girl, nothing would make me happier than making pasta from scratch with my grandmother. I'm a very creative person, so I just loved this project. It made my head spin for a while, because I wasn't sure how to do it. But it was very easy, since there were no complicated ingredients, just the pasta and a big pot of boiling water.

And there I am, trying to teach it to my granddaughters. They are very excited, too. Hopefully they will teach me how. Because I would really appreciate it! I hope they don't mind me saying this. Not everyone in the family likes their pasta made with a lot of ingredients. So I may or may not have to stop making them. For now, we'll just have fun with it and see what happens.
