Salad

Description

Sometimes, nothing is better than a fresh, green salad! My grandfather preferred spinach leaves with just a drizzle of oil, and he lived to be a grandfather. If you like fresh produce, I highly recommend this as the best salad. I love it, or any greens that grow in your area. Eat it plain or dress it up with some oil and vinegar and it's a great salad to enjoy on its own or as a side.

The only thing I don' t like is eating it when it is still hot and steaming hot. That is the worst time to eat it and I would hate to have the same reaction as my grandfather, so be careful when you eat hot food. Keto is very good for people with diabetes. We call it the 'keto diet' because this is how it sounds.

Ingredients

  • Something green
  • Something crunchy
  • Something acidic
  • Something sweet
  • Ranch dressing

Steps

  1. Rinse the greens in cold water.
  2. Chop them up if needed.
  3. Mix everything in a bowl.
  4. Take a picture.
  5. Edit the picture.
  6. Post the picture.
  7. Douse in ranch dressing and eat with a spoon.
